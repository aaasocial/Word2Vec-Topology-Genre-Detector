## Source evaluation

We evaluate every source against three roles: (a) **text fetch** — provides clean public-domain text bytes for training; (b) **curation** — provides candidate-title shortlists ranked by external consensus; (c) **labelling** — provides genre/subject labels we accept as ground truth. The most opinionated finding (per D-03): **Goodreads and LoC are CURATION-ONLY**, not text-fetch, not labelling — they tell us what titles are worth looking for, then we look those titles up in Gutenberg/Standard Ebooks/HathiTrust for the actual text.

v1 forces a single genre label per book. <one or two paragraphs covering:

 - explainability impact: per-track contribution analysis (DEPTH-05) generalises; nearest-neighbours (DEPTH-04) generalises
