Onboarding tour — 4 steps

✓ locked · copy revised

Step 4 ("Inspect the prediction.") dropped per review — the explainability panel is still reachable from the sidebar, and pulling it into the first-load tour added jargon without a clear payoff. Remaining four steps rewritten to drop the math vocabulary: embedding space, persistent homology, calibrated classifier, filtration, N-dimensional space are all gone. The math still lives in How It Works — the tour is for the surface.

STEP 1 of 4
data-tour-id="scatter-canvas"
Each dot is a word.
Words from 154 books, arranged so similar-meaning words sit close together. Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom, press R to reset. You're seeing a 3D version of something that lives in higher dimensions — close enough to explore.
dropped: "embedding space" · "3D projection" · "the real geometry"

STEP 2 of 4
data-tour-id="genre-select"
Light up a genre.
Pick one — its signature words brighten, the common ones fade. Brightness shows how strongly a word belongs to that genre vs the others. Slide through individual books in that genre and watch the pattern shift, book by book.
dropped: "TF-IDF weight" · "distinctive vocabulary"
44 words · interaction
STEP 3 of 4
data-tour-id="upload-zone"
Drop a book.
Drag in any .txt file. We compare its shape to each genre's shape and predict what it is — you'll get the three most likely genres with confidence scores, and the book itself shows up in the cloud with its own bright words highlighted.
dropped: "tokenize, embed" · "persistent homology" · "calibrated classifier"
50 words · core action
STEP 4 of 4
data-tour-id="topology-tab"
Two more views worth a look.
Topology shows each genre as a shape, and tracks the holes that survive as you zoom out — a fingerprint the classifier actually uses. Compare puts two genres side-by-side. Both work from the full geometry, not the 3D view you've been rotating.
dropped: "H₁ persistence image" · "loops persist" · "filtration tightens" · "N-dimensional space"
48 words · close

AFTER · Step 4 — revised

Empty states — 4 surfaces

✓ all approved

All four empty states approved in review. Copy and ghost-scatter visual treatment as drawn in the design doc; below is the copy locked for handoff. Conceptual icon per surface stays the ghost-scatter pattern — never a generic "no data."

A · Pre-upload UploadZone

Headline: Drop a book to classify

Sub: .txt · ≤5MB · ≥500 words

Helper: We chop it up, compare its shape to each genre, and predict — usually under 12s.

Icon: ghost-marker showing where the upload will land in the scatter

B · Compare — no genres selected

Headline: Pick two genres to compare

Sub: Shared color scale · same projection · synchronised camera

Helper: Compare reveals where two genres' vocabularies overlap and where they diverge — useful for the merged keys (gothic_horror, speculative).

Icon: two ghost-scatters, one per panel, each in its own genre color at 25% opacity

C · Classification failure

Headline pattern: "We couldn't [verb] this file" — never "Error occurred"

410 Gone: "This result expired — upload the file again to recompute."

503 (calibration missing): "Top genre still shown; probability bars are degraded."

<500 words: "Try a longer book — at least 500 words."

D · ClassificationExplain — pre-upload

Headline: Upload a book first.

Sub: This panel will show the five nearest training books, how topology and vocabulary contributed, and the words that drove it — once there's a verdict to explain.

Icon: three ghost rows mirroring the three real sub-panels

§ 04

Help menu + tour library

✓ locked

Help menu lives in the header as a "?" icon to the right of "How It Works," left of the gear. Theme toggle (3-state segmented control: Light · System · Dark) lives inside the dropdown so we're not adding two new top-right controls. Tour is built hand-rolled, ~150 LoC, no external dep.

Help menu · A — Header dropdown

  • Trigger: ? icon in header, between "How It Works" and ⚙
  • Items: Replay tour · How It Works · Keyboard shortcuts · theme segmented control · GitHub ↗
  • Anchor: data-tour-id="help-menu" (registered for future tour additions, not used in v1 tour)
  • State key: preferencesStore.lastHelpOpen — closes on outside click or Esc

Tour · Hand-rolled overlay

  • TourProvider — Zustand slice in preferencesStore: tourActive, tourStep, tourCompleted
  • <TourCard /> — fixed-position card, reads TOUR_STEPS[step]; no-ops if findAnchor returns null
  • <TourSpotlight /> — soft dim + glow, not a hard mask cutout (avoids R3F z-index quirks)
  • useTourFirstLoad() — flips tourActive: true on first render if tourCompleted false
  • Playwright smoke test in CI iterates TOUR_STEPS, asserts each findAnchor() is non-null
