# Plan 12-04 — Submit a Text → The Reading

## Objective
The upload → verdict flow, reading-room idiom, on the **real** pipeline (`useClassify` SSE + `useExplain`), not the prototype's simulated delay.

**Submit a Text (§6.6):** 2-col — left the reading desk (`data-tour-id="reading-desk"`): "foolscap" framed textarea with word count, "Generate a reading →" / "or upload a .txt", 3 sample-passage buttons; right an empty-state panel ("The reading appears here") + privacy note. Submitting kicks the real classify job and routes to verdict on completion (show the staged pipeline progress while it runs).

**The Reading / verdict (§6.7):** breadcrumb + share/print; 2-col — left the essay (label, 38px title, indented paragraphs with footnotes⁴⁵⁶, a "Probability fix" bar chart from the real top-N, a Notes block) · right a catalog card for the text (provisional shelfmark, Verdict + Confidence rendered with the **L-13 voice rule**: <0.80 → "marginal", never "wrong"; two-track centroid/topology contributions from `useExplain`), a "Where it landed" **SVG** mini-plate with a dashed pin, and "Nearest five works".
