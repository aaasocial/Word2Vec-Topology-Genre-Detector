# Plan 12-06 — Guide + guided tour

## Objective
The two onboarding systems that replace the Phase 11 chain (D-U2).

**Guide (§6.9, RR-07):** right side-sheet (480 wide), header "Reader's aid / The Guide", 3 tabs:
- 01 Welcome — what this is + a "you can do three things" card.
- 02 How to wander — prominent "Begin the guided tour" button (6 stops · ~2 min · skippable) + itinerary; launching closes the sheet.
- 03 How it works — 5 method steps each with a **live figure** (`guide_figures.jsx`): FigWordEmbed, FigCentroid, FigTopology (auto-sweep ε + drag-scrub), FigProjection, FigVerdict. Figures **must render at rest** (L-08 / §7 — not gated on entrance animations / document timeline; degrade to a valid static frame in a background tab).
Auto-opens once per browser (localStorage `rr.guide.seen.v1`); reopen from masthead "Guide". Backdrop click closes.

**Guided tour (§6.10 / L-09/L-10, RR-08):** 6 stops navigating the real screens — ① plate (collection) ② catalog rail (collection) ③ catalog card (card) ④ topology plate (topology — pre-selects Mystery) ⑤ study pickers (study) ⑥ reading desk (upload). Each: navigate route → poll for `[data-tour-id]` → frame with **four dim panels** (`rgba(38,33,27,0.46)`) + `1.5px accent` frame + corner ticks → pin margin card to the opposite quadrant (STOP n/6, title, body, End tour · ← Back · Next →). ←/→/Esc; missing anchor → wait ~700ms then advance.

Prototype: `guide.jsx`, `guide_figures.jsx`, `tour.jsx`. Screenshots: `09-guide-how-it-works.png`, `10-guided-tour.png`. Reuses/extends `tour/anchors.ts` + `TourOverlay`/`TourProvider` (replace the Phase 10 single-glow spotlight with the four-panel reading-room spotlight; replace the 4-step TOUR_STEPS with these 6).
