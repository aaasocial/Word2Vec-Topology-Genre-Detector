# Plan 12-03 — Catalog card + Comparative Study

## Objective
Two related browse screens over existing data.

**Catalog card (§6.3, RR-03):** breadcrumb (Collection › Genre › Title), 3-col carrel — region-siblings rail · plate **detail** (selected book highlighted; the 4 nearest drawn with dashed accent leader lines + labels) · the letterpress **catalog card** aside (`border-top:4px double ink` + hard offset shadow + punch-hole; shelfmark, title 24, author·year, key/value grid [Genre, Words, Vocab, UMAP-x/y], "See also" links, "Driving vocabulary" chips, "Five nearest" list with color dot + cosine distance).
